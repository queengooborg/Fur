Go to the Start Menu
Right-click on Computer
Click Properties
On the top-left, click on advanced system settings
Click on the Advanced tab
Click on Environment Variables
On the list of "System Variables", scroll down until you see the "Path" variable, then click on it
Click "Edit"
Scroll to the end of the "Variable value" and type the following: ;C:\Python27
Click Okay on all of the windows
Close the computer properties
In the Project Fur folder, double-click on "finish.bat" (it's under installer-assets > python-installer)
